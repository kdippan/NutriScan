Quick FAQ

How accurate is the Nutri-Score?
The Nutri-Score is based on data directly from the Open Food Facts database, calculating the balance between healthy nutrients (fiber, protein) and those to limit (sugars, sodium, saturated fats).
Where does the data come from?
All data is sourced from Open Food Facts, an open, crowdsourced database of food products globally under the Open Database License.
