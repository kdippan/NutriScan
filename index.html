Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Nutri-Score?
The Nutri-Score is based on data from the Open Food Facts database. It scientifically calculates the balance between healthy nutrients (fiber, protein) and those to limit (sugars, sodium, saturated fats).
Does NutriScan work offline?
While live scanning requires an internet connection to fetch fresh product data, your scan history is saved locally on your device for quick reference anytime.
Where does the data come from?
All data is sourced from Open Food Facts, an open, crowdsourced database of food products globally.
